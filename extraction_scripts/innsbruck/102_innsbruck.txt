$I              Jhesu M=l=iiij=c= iiij=xx=vij
$I Ryght worschyppffull syrs and my reuerent mastyrs, afftyr all dew
$I recommendacon precedynge I louly recommend mee vnto yowr
$I mastyrschyppis. Fyrddyrmore, plese hytt yowre mastyrschyppis
$I to vnderstond that I am commvn to Calles yn saffte, thancked be
$I allmyghty Godd, for I was never yn soo grett jebardy comyng owte
$I of Flaunddyrs yn my lyeffe, ffor men a warre lyinge be the waye
$I waytynge for Englyschemen; and allsoo I and my cumpany was
$I arestyd ij days at Dunckyrke, but ffor Syr Jamus Tyrrellys sake
$I wee were lett go. And soo, syr, the world goyth marvyllyusly yn
$I Flaunddyrs now, for hytt ys open warre betwyxte Gaunte and the
$I Kynge of Romayns, etc. Syr, as ffor makyng over of yowre mony,
$I syns thys trubbull began I cowde nott make over a peny, savyng
$I an xlviij li. ster., wherof I schall send yow the byllys at the nexte
$I passage. But syr, John delowppis schewyd mee at my departyng
$I that I schuld wryte unto yowr mastyrschyppis to vnderstond

$I wheder there schall be any jebardy to brynge warys owte of the
$I Est partyes ynto Ynglond now ffro hensforth or nott, as he sup_posyth
$I that that acte of þe contrary schall be put yn susspence
$I for dyuersse causys; wherffor, syr, he avysyth yow to bestowe yowre
$I mony yn grosse warys now betymys at þis Barow martt; yn syche

$I your mastyrschyppis and schyppyd hytt yn Spaynysche schyppis
