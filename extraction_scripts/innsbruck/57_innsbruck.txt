$I Ryght trusty Cosyn, I comaunde me to you: and where as hit was
$I agreed by you and my councell at your beyng at Dorchester byfore
$I Crystmasse that Richard Tomyowe, consyderyng the gode service that
$I he hath don for my husbonde and me in dayes passed and the charges
$I that he must do for me here after, shulde be made sure of landes and
$I tenementez to the yerely value of xxti marke. And he ys agreed to take
$I too lytill Manours in Corunwall, one called Wynnyanton and the other
$I called Kenell, of the value by the yere of xij. li.: of the whiche I pray
$I you, Cosyn, to speke to my cosyn Syr Richard Harecourte and Edward
$I Grymston that betweene you to sealle hym a dede that William Menwyn_nek
$I other Richard Reynolds shall brynge you of the said Maners terme
$I of his lyve: for he woll not procede no furder in my maters in to the
$I tyme he be made sure of the same, whiche were to me a grete hurt, as
$I ye understonde. And that this be done as my speciall trust is in you:
$I and our lorde have you in his blessed kepyng. Wrytten at Excestre the
$I xxvijth day off Janyver.

$I By your Cosyn Dame Kateryne Arundell.
$I To my ryght worshipfull Cosyn, Thomas Stoner, Esquyer.
