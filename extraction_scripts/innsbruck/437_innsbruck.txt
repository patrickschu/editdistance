$I    The alteration of my mothers former resolutions (who is

<p. 95

$I now resolv'd against ever moving from hence) puts mee upon
$I some thoughts w=ch= were allmost quite out of my head; but you
$I may be sure I shall determine nothing that does not tend as
$I much to your reall happiness as lies in my power. I have there_fore
$I sent you this letter to prepare you for a remove first hither,
$I & afterwards as fate shall direct which is (I find) the true
$I disposer of things whatever wee attribute to wisdome or
$I providence, bee therefore in a readiness upon the first notice
$I from mee to put that in execution w=ch= I shall first informe you
$I particularly of  let me have an answar & dispatch this messenger
$I quickly.
$I    God bless you
$I                              yours
$I                            Rochester
$I These for the C. of R.
