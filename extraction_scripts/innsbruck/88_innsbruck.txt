$I October [1480?]
$I Worshipfull cosyn I recomaund me hertely to yov and I pray
$I yov, cosyn, to se Leyston delyver my cosyn Makeney x li. nov or
$I that yt vylle plese yov to forbere the seyd x li. and take yt of hym
$I thys halluntyde. Leyston hath sent me vord by Hypvorth that
$I he vylle be with me this halun eve with the sum of xx li., vere of I
$I vylle x li. be delyuered nov and I pray yov cosyn, let John For[d]

<p. 16

$I be her nov with alle suche diligens in his reseyt and Jhesus preserve
$I yov, to my power

$I Wyllm Stonore, Kt.
