$I 10 July 1583.
$I Mr. Broughton, I undrstand by M'. Wright that he hath re_ceaved
$I nothing of his Annuitie sins my coming into Yorkeshire.
$I I do assure my self that yf you will pay him it will not be misliked
$I of any; and therefore I desire you that you will discharge that wch
$I is due unto him. Thus hopinge you will do yor endeavor herein,
$I I bid you hartely farewell.    Yorke, this xth of July 1583.
$I Yors  
$I (Signed) R. ESSEX.
