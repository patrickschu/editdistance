$I MY LORDE DAKERS
$I I comend me to zou as hartely as I can, and I have
$I seen zour vryteng and ondarstande at length, and I
$I parcayve that ze ar nouht sykerly informyd in what
$I stat I stand in, for ze trow yat I may pas vhar ever I
$I vyl vyshe, is nowht trw; but yes berrar can schow
$I zou ye trowht of all, and vhat my mynd is, and how
$I I am constraynd to doo a gan my vyl. And I pray
$I zou gyf hm kredens as ze valde doo to my selfe, for
$I it is owr lang to vryt, for I have gret trast in yes
$I man. And send me zour uter mynd and ansuer in
$I all thyng: and God kype zou. Vryten vy=th= my hand
$I yes Monday.
$I Zour frend
$I MARGARET R.
