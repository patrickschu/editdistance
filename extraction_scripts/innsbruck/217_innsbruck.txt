$I 25 January 1584.
$I Mr. Broughton, I pray you cause King wth as much speade
$I as he can to make me two suits of apparell a long cloke and send me
$I six black rought falling bands when Ed. Meuxell comes to London
$I I pray you help him to but a cast of tassels. Thus wth comendacions
$I to your self, your partner, and all at Warwyk Inne, I bid you fare_well.
$I Lanfey this xxvth of January 1584.
$I Yours assured
$I (Signed) R. ESSEX.
