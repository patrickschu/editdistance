<Margery Hampden Stonor to William Stonor
<The Stonor Letters and Papers 1290–1483, ed. Charles Lethbridge Kingsford, Vol. II
<Letter 186, p. 28.

$I Worchypfull Cossyne, I recomaund me unto yow, and I pray yow 
$I hartely, cossyne, to come heder thys nyte or to-morow by tymesse, both 
$I for þe mater off my nefew, and also for þe mater that I desyred yow, 
$I asse my truste yn yow, or asse I may do thynge to yowr plesur, þe 
$I weche to do I wold, be glad, God knowthe, ho have yow yn ys kepinge.
$I Yowres M. Hampdun.
$I To my worchippfull Cossyn, W. Stonor, in haste.
