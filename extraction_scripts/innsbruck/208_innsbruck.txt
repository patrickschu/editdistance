$I 2 Novembris 1580.
$I Mr. Broughton, I have receaved l'res from my L. of Hunt_ingdon
$I how that he myndeth to write to my L. Tresorer that he may
$I have my Company in the north this Chr'as, to wch I wold be willing.
$I As for all things I want Merick is able to certifie you. I loke for my

<p. 22

$I books. Thus wth commendacons to yor self yor partner, brothers,
$I sisters and frends, quos omnes recensere longum esset, I take my
$I leave.   Cambridge this 2 of November, 1580.
$I Yors
$I  (Signed) R. ESSEX.
