$I Madam
$I    I am extreamly troubled for the sickness of y=r= son as well in
$I consideration of the affliction it gives you, as the dearness I
$I have for him myself; you have I heare done mee the favour to
$I expect me long in the Country where I intended to have bin long
$I agoe, but Court affaires are more hardly sollicited now then ever,

$I y=rs= too, I was forc't to stay somthing longer here till I had

$I hinder mee from what I heartily desire w=ch= is to waite on y=r= La=sp=
$I at Adderbury
