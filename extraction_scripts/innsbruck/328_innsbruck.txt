$I      To my deare husband S=r= Robart Harley, Knight of the Bathe.
$I S=r=  Docter Barker has put my sister into a cours of ientell fisek,
$I which I hope by God's bllsing will doo her much good. My sister
$I giues you thankes for seending him to her. I pray you remember
$I that I recken the days you are away; and I hope you are nowe
$I well at Heariford, wheare it may be, this letter will put you in minde
$I of me, and let you know, all your frinds heare are well; and all
$I the news I can seend you is, that my Lo. Brooke is nowe at Beae_thams
$I Court. My hope is to see you heare this day senet, or
$I to-morrowe senet, and I pray God giue vs a happy meeting, and
$I presarfe you safe; which will be the great comfort of
$I      Your most true affectionat wife,           BRILLIANA HARLEY.
$I Ragley: the 30 of Sep. 1625.
