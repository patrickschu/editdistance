$I By the Quene.
$I We wol and charge you that ye deliver or do to be delivered unto 
$I oure trusty and welbeloved Syr William Stoner, knyght, or unto the
$I bringer herof in his name, one buk of this season: to be taken of our 
$I gifte within our Forest of Blakmore, any restrainte or commaundement 
$I to you directed to the contrary herof notwithstanding. And thise oure 
$I letters shalbe your sufficient warrant animpst us in that behalve. Yeven 
$I under oure signet at my Lordes Castell of Wyndesore the xix day of 
$I August the xxj yere of my said lordes Reigne.

<p. 128

$I To our trusty and welbeloved the maistre fforster of oure fforest of 
$I Blakmore, and to all other fforsters and kepers there in his absence, 
$I and to every of tham.
