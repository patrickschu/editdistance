$I   Ryught interly whelbelouyd and my syngeler good brother,
$I I recomende me wnto you in as louyng whys as hartte con thynke.
$I Plese hyt yow to wndyrstonde at the maky[ng] of thys howr father
$I and mother, my godfather Maryon and whe aull [wher] at London
$I in good heyll, thankyd be the good Loorde. Syr, I haue bene in
$I Cottyssowlde and bohut for hus xxv=c= pellys, pryse le C of xv=c=:
$I iij li., and of a M=l=: heuery C iij li. iij s. iiij d., and I haue payd and
$I a mwste pay yyth[in] thys v days in parte of p[a]yment of thes
$I fellys and for caryayge, xl li. and aboue, and I mwste pay to
$I Wylliam Mydwynter at Bartyllmewys tyd xx li., and at Hallontyd
$I xx li. for the forsayd fellys. Syr, I pray yow haue theys dayes in
$I rememerans, my powr honeste lyes ther apon. And at my com_myngys
$I howte of Cottyssowlde apone a schorte pwrpos howr father
$I has schypyd xvij sarpellerys of hys wooll that whos packyd at Nor_lache
$I syn Ester, and ther [ys] vj of theme myddyll, and that ys aull
$I the myddyll woll of that soorte. I know hyt whell, ther come not
$I better myddyll woll of howr father thys vij 3eyr; and at the next
$I schyppyng howr father wyll schype the remenand of good whooll
$I of thys sorte, and hawlle hys fellys, and so wyll I howrys. And
$I I have received ij lettyrs frome you, whon of howr brother

$I Robarde, and therin whos of hys own hande contanyng iiij li.
$I starlyng payabull the iiij day of [June]. I pray God send ws good
$I payment, and another Edwhard Lenawllys, the qweche I do whell
$I wndyrston[d]. I pwrpos be the gras of God to be at Lontelays
$I whoddyng on Sonday next, and my godfather to. Syr, heyr ys
$I yowr blake hors and youer gray at London: thay ar in good plyte.

$I Ther ys no mane byd no mony for them, and thay stond you to
