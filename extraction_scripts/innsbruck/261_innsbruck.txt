$I    M Hinchlow, the company told me y=u= wear expected thear
$I yesterday to conclude about thear comming over, or goinge to
$I Oxford.I have not only labord my own play, which shall be
$I ready before they come over, but given Cyrill Tourneur an act
$I of y=e= Arreignment of London to write, y=t= we may have y=t= like_wise

$I wher I deale otherways then to y=r= content, may I and myne
$I want ffryndship in distress! so, relying one y=r= favor, which shall
$I never reap loss by me, I rest
$I                                at y=r= commaund,

$I   5=o= June, 1613.
