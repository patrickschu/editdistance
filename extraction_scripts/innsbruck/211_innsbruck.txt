$I ult May 1582.
$I Mr. Broughton, I undrstand by this bearer Richard Powell
$I my ffather's old servant that he holdeth a litle tenemt of me in Lynols
$I of vijs.iiijd. rent and there is there a farme called Sherrats ferme
$I wch the widowe that holdeth it is not desirous to sue for. I pray
$I you let him obteyne so much favor that he may take it for his money
$I before any other wch is his only sute. William Powell hath bene
$I here wth me cravinge some help or relief at my hands for whom I
$I would do for his Mr's. sake any thinge that I could and therefore
$I considering his want I desire you if you can spye any such things
$I wherby he might have some stay of livinge that you will provide
$I for him. Thus wth 26 dosen of comendacons to all my ffrends in
$I Staffordshire I bid you farewell.   Yorke, this last of May 1582.
$I Yors
$I (Signed) R. ESSEX.
$I I promysed Mr. Aston to do for Powell who requested me in his
$I lif tyme. I specially commend my sel to the house of Bliethfield.
