$I MY KIND DOG
$I Your Letter hath bin acceptable to me; I rest
$I alreadie assured of your carefulnesse. Yow maye tell
$I your Maister that the King of Dennemarke hath sent 
$I me tuelf faire mares, and as the bringer of them assures

<p. 101

$I me, all great with foles, which I intend to put into 
$I Byfield Parke, where being the other daye a hunting 
$I I could find but verie few deare, but greate store
$I of other cattle as I shall tell your Maister my self when 
$I I see him. I hope to meete you all at Woodstock at
$I the time apponited, till when I wish you all happines
$I and contentment.
$I ANNA R.
$I I thank yow for your paines taken in re_membring
$I the King for the pailing of me
$I parke. I will doe you anie service I can.
$I To
$I Sir George Villiers.
