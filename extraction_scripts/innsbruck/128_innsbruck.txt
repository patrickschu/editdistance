<Mary the French Queen to Thomas King Henry VIII (1514),
<Original letters illustrative of English history, ed. Henry Ellis, vol. 2,
<Letter 43, pp. 123-125.
<[MS. COTTON, CALIG. D. VI. fol. 106. Org.]

$I my most derest and ryt entierly belowyde Lord
$I and brothare, yn my most humble wys I recommande

<p. 124

$I me unto yowr Grace, shawynge unto yowr Grace that
$I I do a pa . . . . by my Lord my howsbande that
$I y[ou] ar playsayde and contentyde that he shale re_sorde
$I on to yowr presence at swche tyme as yowr
$I Grace shale be at hys maner of Donyngton, wherby I
$I se wele he hys merwosly rejoysyd and moche com_fortyd
$I that yt hathe lyked yowr Grace so to be play_sayd;
$I for the wyche yowr specyale goodnys to hym
$I showyd yn that be halfe, and for sondry and many oder
$I yowr kyndnes, as wele to me as to hym showed and
$I gewyn yn dyvers cawsys, I most humbly thanke yowr
$I Grace assewryng yow that for the same I accompt my
$I self as moche bonden un to yowr Grace as ewer swster
$I was to brother: and accordyng ther un to I shale to the
$I beste of my powr dowryng my lyef, endver myselfe as
$I ferre as in me shale be possyble to do the thyng that
$I shale stond with yowr playsowr, and yf it had be tyme
$I convenyente to yowr Grace hade be ther wythe pleas_syd
$I I wolde most gladly have accompanyd my sayd
$I Lord yn thys yowrnay. Bwt I trowst that bowthe
$I I and my sayd Lord shal se yow acordyng as yowr
$I Grace worte yn yowr laste Letters unto my sayde
$I Lord, wych ys the thyng that I dessyr more to opteyn
$I than all the honor off the Worlde. And thws I be_seche
$I owre Lorde to send unto yow, my most dereste
$I and enterly be lowyd brother and Lord, long and pros_perows

<p. 125

$I lyfe with the fole accomplyshment of al yowr
$I honorable dessyrs, most humbly prayeng yowr Grace
$I that I may be humbly recomanded unto my most derest
$I and beste be lowyd suster the Quene Grace, and to the
$I Quene off Scottys my welbelowyd suster, trowstyng
$I that she be asserteyned frome yowr Grace of the pros_perows
$I estate and helthe of my everly beloved ny[phews]
$I the prences, to home I pray God send long lyfe. Frome
$I Letheryng in Swf the ix. day off Septembur, by the
$I hand of yowr lowyng suster
$I MARY QUENE OFF FRANCE.
