$I My most kynde and lovyng brother, I humbly re_co-mand
$I me vnto yowr Grace, thanyng yow internly
$I of yowr comfortable Letters; besechyng yowr grace

<p. 122

$I most humbly now so to contenwe toward me and my
$I frendes, as owr spessyale trowst ys yn yowr Grace; and
$I that yt may layke you w=t= all convennynte delygence
$I to sende for me that I may shortely se yowr grace 
$I wyhce ys the thyng that I most dissyr yn thys world 
$I and I and all myn ys at yowr gracs commandmente
$I and playsayr. At Parys the vj. day of Marche.
$I by yowr lowyng
$I suster
$I MARY
$I To the Kings Grace
$I this be delivered.
