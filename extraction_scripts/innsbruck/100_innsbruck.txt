$I              Jhesu M=l=iiij=c= iiij=xx=vij
$I Ryght worschyppffull syrs and my reuerent mastyrs, afftyr all dew
$I recomendacon precedynge I lowly recommend mee unto yowre
$I masterschyppys. Fyrdyrmore, plese hytt yowre mastyrschyppys to
$I understond that I haue receyued yowre lettyr datyd at London the
$I ffyrst day of September, be the tenour therof I understond yowre
$I mastyrschyppys hath take upp the exchaunge of John Raynold,
$I mercer lx li. ster., payabull the xxv day of thys month, and of
$I Deago Decastron, Spaynard, odyr lx li. ster., payabull the xxvj day
$I of the same month, the whych schall be both content at the day.
$I And as ffor mastyr Lowys More, Lomberd, ys payd and I hawe

$I the byll. Hys atorney ys a wranglyng felow: he wold non odyr
$I mony but Nemyng grotys, etc. Item, syr, I understond be yowre
$I sayd letter that yowr mastyrschyppus hath receyued noo wryttyng
$I from mee syns Addlynton was here, wherof I marvell, for I wrate
$I ij letters to yow whyle I was at Brugys, specyfying of divers matters
$I of Flaunders. The ton was sent from Calles be Jamus Jarfford,
$I mercer; the toder be Peryman, packer of clyfte wullys. He ys logyd
$I at the Crosse Keye. Syr, I hawe ben at Calles thys ix days, and
$I abowte the latter end of thys weke I purpose to Brugyswardde
$I agayne, and I leue Thomas Colton and Roberd Hubberd atorneys
$I tyll I com, and I schall leve wyth Thomas Colton vj li. fl for
$I yowre mastyrschypp yff ye com, and as for yowre black box,
$I I leve hytt wyth Thomas Graunger. Syr, as for goyng ynto
$I Flaundyrs, ys goode inow3the as yett, but all the jebardy ys yn
$I comyng home, for and yf owre men of war take ther ffyscher, as
$I I ffeyr they wyll, ther wyll be many Englyschemen stoppyd yn
$I Flaunders; and allsoo the towne of Donkyrk ys nott content, and
$I that wee schall well know yff soo be that they mete wyth any
$I merchauntys of substaunce. And syr, as for a lettyr of atorney
$I under the Stappell sell, I can hawe no wythowte I bryng a letter
$I of atorney from yowrys mastyrschyppys owte of Ynglond, for
$I yowre mastyrschyp hath ben at Calles diuersse tymes syns I was
$I entryd yowre atorney, wherffor yowre presens hath deffetyd that
$I entre; and a letter of atorney ys nedffull now at thys tym[e].
$I Item, syr receyued of John Saunders a letter wheryn
$I enclossyd ij letters of payment, on of Benyngne Decason derectyd
$I to Gabryell Desurle and Peter Sauly, Genovo[ise], conteynyng
$I lx li. ster. Item, anoder letter of Jacob Van De Base derectyd to
$I Anthony Corsy and Marcus Strossy, Spaynearddys, conteynyng
$I xxix li. xix s. iiij d. ster., etc. Item, syr, yowre wull ys awarddyd
$I be the sarpler that I cast owte last, etc. Item, syr, thys same day
$I yowre mastyrschypp ys alectyd and poyntyd here b[e] the courtt
$I oon of the xxviij the whych schall asyste the Mayer of t[he]
$I Stappell now at thys parliament tyme, wheresomeuer hytt be
$I holden, and to labore serten matters for the Place wherof ys
$I ynstructons sent to the Mayer be wryttyng, etc. Item, syr, I send

$I rekenyng and hys agree, etc. No more vnto yowre mastyrschypp at
